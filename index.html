Our Vision

To restore, reconcile, and renew communities by inspiring a generation of peacemakers and equipping vulnerable children and youth to live fully into God’s purposes

Our Mission

To express God’s love and care by discipling, developing, and delivering basic needs for vulnerable children and youth in Uganda.

Spiritual Statement

“Love is a safe place of shelter, for it never stops believing the best for others. Love never takes failure as defeat, for it never gives up.” 1 Corinthians 13:7

Our Core Programs

Child Rights and Child Protection

  • Freedom from abuse and neglect
  • Provision of basic needs: nutrition, clothing, education, physical and mental health care
  • Family reunification and reconciliation

Psychological and Emotional Healing

  • Individual, family, and group counselling
  • Trauma processing and healing
  • Social-emotional development
  • Peace-making, conflict-resolution, and reconciliation

Spiritual Development

  • Prayer
  • Bible teaching
  • Discipleship and mentoring
  • Worship

Learning and Education

  • Foundational education of vulnerable children and youth, especially focused on literacy and numeracy
  • Learning opportunities across diverse learning styles using targeted teaching strategies

Life Skills Development

  • Develop basic life skills
  • Develop social communication skills, for example, aggression management
  • Enhance social-emotional skills
  • Empowerment of youth in advocacy and leadership

Capacity Building

  • Economic empowerment of youth and their families
  • Training in income generating activities
  • Talent development

Community Engagement

  • Child rights and human rights advocacy
  • Volunteers from the community
  • Community service projects

DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY AND APPROACH

Eleazar African Foundation works alongside local organizations, community leaders, and volunteers to support the development of Lira in an intentional proactive, coordinated, and strategic manner with the meaningful participation of vulnerable children and youth, who are often neglected and marginalized. This is done in collaborative partnership with caregivers, community members, community leaders, and partners locally, nationally, and internationally

Furthermore

Eleazar African Foundation serves as an advocate and voice for orphans and vulnerable children and youth. Often these children and youth are an invisible or oppressed minority in their communities. We strive to empower them to share their stories and inform the community regarding their challenges, including poverty, mistreatment, and disability, and how to effectively address these obstacles and limitations. Each child and youth has a unique and powerful story. Simply sharing their stories moves the community to see these children and youth from a different perspective, to develop compassion and empathy, and to actively care for one of their own.

About Us

Eleazar African Foundation is a Christian non-profit organization registered as a Ugandan Foreign NGO in October 2020. Our core work is part of the arc of the Kingdom narrative that is centrally about reconciling all things, making right the injustices in the world, and restoring all of creation to health, beauty, and freedom.
