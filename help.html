WAYS TO HELP

Eleazar African Foundation believes every child deserves a bright future! With God's guidance, we strive to ensure children in our care, receive the support to grow up healthy, spiritually-informed, intellectually stimulated, and emotionally secure. However, we can’t do this important work without your support!

Volunteering

Join us in making a meaningful impact! By volunteering with our organization, you can help bring hope to our community. Your time and effort can provide essential support, create lasting change, and inspire others to contribute. Whether it’s an hour a week or more, every bit helps. Together, we can make a difference. Sign up today and be part of something truly special. Your community needs you!

Give a one-time gift

Your one-time gift can make a world of difference in the life of a child or family! You are helping us to build up, encourage, and support orphans and vulnerable children in Uganda!

Give a monthly donation

When you donate monthly to Eleazar African Foundation, rest assured you're creating footprints of lasting change in children’s lives! You are helping us set the foundation for peace-makers and agents of change for Uganda.

Make a memorial gift

Create Your Own Fundraiser

Start a fundraising campaign to support our orphans and vulnerable children in Uganda.

Whether young or old, you can help raise awareness and support for Eleazar African Foundation, simply by creating a fundraiser or book drive. Thank you for bringing us into your homes and communities!

About Us

Eleazar African Foundation is a Christian non-profit organization registered as a Ugandan Foreign NGO in October 2020. Our core work is part of the arc of the Kingdom narrative that is centrally about reconciling all things, making right the injustices in the world, and restoring all of creation to health, beauty, and freedom.
