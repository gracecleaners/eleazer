WAYS TO HELP

Eleazar African Foundation believes every Child deserves a future. A reason we do whatever it takes every day as God guides us to ensure Children under our care & support grow up healthy, spiritually informed, learning and safe. Especially those in high vulnerability. However, we can’t do this important work without your support.

Volunteering

Join us in making a meaningful impact! By volunteering with our organization, you can help bring hope to our community. Your time and effort can provide essential support, create lasting change, and inspire others to contribute. Whether it’s an hour a week or more, every bit helps. Together, we can make a difference. Sign up today and be part of something truly special. Your community needs you!

About Us

Eleazar African Foundation is a Christian non-profit organization registered as a Ugandan Foreign NGO in October 2020. Our core work is part of the arc of the Kingdom narrative that is centrally about reconciling all things, making right the injustices in the world, and restoring all of creation to health, beauty, and freedom.
